StarThink’s Innovation: ScanMate

A mobile app that promotes healthy eating habits among students through fun food scanning, engaging lessons, and rewarding activities.

About ScanMate

ScanMate is a mobile application designed for Grade 4 to 6 students that encourages healthy eating habits through fun and interactive experiences. By scanning their meals, students can earn points, discover exciting nutrition facts, and learn how to make smarter food choices in a more enjoyable way.

Mission

“To encourage kids to eat smarter through technology and fun learning.”

Vision

“To create a generation of students who value health and nutrition as part of their daily life.”
